Stephen Curry

The NBA Superstar

Here's a timeline of Stephen Curry's life:

  • 1988 - Born in Akron, Ohio
  • 2006 - Graduates from Charlotte Christian School
  • 2006 - Attends Davidson College, where he excels in basketball
  • 2009 - Leads Davidson Wildcats to the Elite Eight of the NCAA Tournament
  • 2009 - Drafted by the Golden State Warriors as the 7th overall pick
  • 2015 - Wins his first NBA Championship with the Warriors, named NBA MVP
  • 2016 - Breaks his own record for most three-pointers made in a single NBA season
  • 2017 - Leads the Warriors to their second NBA Championship in three years
  • 2021 - Becomes the all-time leading scorer in Warriors franchise history
  • 2022 - Surpasses Ray Allen for the most career three-pointers made in NBA history

"Stephen Curry's impact on the game of basketball and his record-breaking achievements have forever changed the way the sport is played."

-- NBA Commissioner Adam Silver
